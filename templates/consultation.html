  • About Us

    Rewrite your period story through MASIKA. We make menstrual health affordable, sustainable, and accessible, while breaking taboos and empowering every person with dignity and confidence. MASIKA is about turning periods from a barrier into a story of strength, pride, and freedom.

  • FAQs
    What is Masika Health?

    Masika Health is a comprehensive menstrual health platform using AI to provide personalized insights. We help you track your cycle, understand symptoms, and manage your health affordably and sustainably.

    How does the AI analysis work?

    Our AI analyzes the cycle data and symptoms you log, and can optionally extract key values from medical reports you upload. It then generates personalized summaries, dietary suggestions, and recommendations to help you feel your best.

    Is my health data safe?

    Absolutely. Your privacy is our top priority. All personal health data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your identifiable information with third parties without your explicit consent.

    What are Masika Points?

    Masika Points are our way of rewarding you for consistently tracking your health. You can earn points for logging your data regularly and use them to get discounts on our products and services.

    How can I consult a doctor?

    You can connect with certified healthcare professionals directly through the 'Consult' section in our app. Simply navigate there using the bottom navigation bar to schedule a virtual consultation.
